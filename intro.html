About Krishna Joshi's Project

This Zomato Clone is designed to mimic the functionalities of the popular food delivery platform, Zomato. Our project aims to provide users with a seamless experience for exploring restaurants, browsing menus, and ordering food online.

With a user-friendly interface and a variety of features, our clone allows you to:

  • Search for restaurants by name or cuisine
  • Browse menus and food items
  • Place orders and make payments
  • Read reviews and ratings
  • Contact customer support for any queries
